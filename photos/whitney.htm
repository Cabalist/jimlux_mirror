Top of Mt Whitney, October,  1986(?), 4419m (14494 ft)
L-> R: John Vafiades, Tom Jedrey, Jim Lux
This was the 2nd snowstorm of the season, so the view wasn't very spectacular here. The view from Trail Crest, on the other hand, was..We did the usual 2 day trip up the trail, Whitney Portal to Trail Camp the first day, leave packs at TC, summit and back down on the second day. This trip was notable for me because I injured my knee about a mile away from the cars on the way back down, and couldn't walk for several days afterwards.
