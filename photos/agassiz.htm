Top of Mt Agassiz (near Bishop Pass), August 1987, 4235m (13,892 ft)
L->R: Dave Untrauer, Jim Lux, John Vafiades, Pete Seemsen
view looks roughly southwest over Sequoia/Kings Canyon National Park. The hike up is sort of a slog from Bishop Pass(12,000 ft), with some Class 2 boulder scrambling to the top. We came down via a couloir to the southwest which empties (eventually) into Dusy Basin, where we had camped.
