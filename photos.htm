These photos were quickly scanned from 8x10 original prints as TIFF files. They were converted to JPEG using Adobe Photoshop at "medium" resolution, so the color isn't great, and neither is the resolution, but then, you'd have to see the original image to do it justice.

Click on one of the thumbnail photos to the left to see a bigger version (typically 5 inches on the long dimension at 75 pixels/inch). The big versions are all about 10-20Kbytes, so they should download in about 10 seconds.
